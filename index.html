Courses We Offer

For scholars who share a common passion for inquiry because of what it can reveal about our world and ourselves, there is opportunity here.

Intermediate

The UMass Dartmouth online Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) program is 100% online and provides a broad and deep knowledge of computer science with an emphasis on software development. The University also offers a full-time, on-campus MSCS program.

College Degree

High quality undergraduate programmes in areas such as accounting, business, marketing and healthcare management, all of which are designed to nurture your potential and help you progress onto a thriving career. You’ll be taught by dedicated lecturers and can help you to become an asset to any company.

Post Graduation

programmes offered to students that have completed their undergraduate studies. While a Master’s Programme is of three years’ duration, on the other hand, A PG Diploma’s duration is of one or two years (maximum). It can be further divided into two or four semesters depending on the university.
